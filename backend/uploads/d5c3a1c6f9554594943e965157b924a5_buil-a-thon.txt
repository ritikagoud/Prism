Executive Summary 
Drishti is an AI-powered crisis intelligence platform that helps emergency responders and 
decision-makers identify, analyze, and manage crisis situations efficiently. The platform 
transforms scattered information into actionable intelligence through AI-driven analysis, 
severity assessment, visualization, and automated reporting. 
By providing a centralized decision-support system, Drishti enables faster response times, 
improved situational awareness, and more effective resource allocation during emergencies. 
 
Problem Statement 
During disasters and emergency situations, critical information is often fragmented across 
multiple sources such as social media platforms, news channels, public reports, and 
emergency communications. 
This information overload creates significant challenges for authorities, including delayed 
incident detection, inaccurate prioritization, and inefficient coordination of response efforts. 
A unified system capable of collecting, analyzing, and presenting crisis intelligence in real 
time is essential for effective emergency management. 
 
Proposed Solution 
Drishti addresses these challenges through an AI-powered crisis intelligence framework. 
The platform collects incoming crisis-related information, processes it using advanced AI 
techniques, evaluates severity levels, and presents actionable insights through an intuitive 
dashboard. Automated report generation further assists stakeholders in making informed 
decisions during critical situations. 
 
Key Features 
Real-Time Crisis Monitoring 
Monitors and processes incoming crisis-related information to maintain up-to-date situational 
awareness. 
AI-Powered Incident Analysis 
Utilizes Large Language Models and Natural Language Processing techniques to analyze 
reports and extract meaningful intelligence. 
Severity Classification 
Automatically categorizes incidents based on urgency, impact, and response priority. 
Interactive Analytics Dashboard 
Provides a centralized view of crisis information, trends, and incident summaries. 
Automated Report Generation 
Generates concise reports that support rapid decision-making and response planning. 
Data Visualization 
Converts complex crisis data into understandable visual insights for better analysis.

System Workflow 
1. Crisis-related information is collected from available data sources. 
2. Incoming data is cleaned, structured, and prepared for analysis. 
3. AI models process the information and identify significant incidents. 
4. The platform evaluates incident severity and priority levels. 
5. Insights are displayed through the analytics dashboard. 
6. Automated reports are generated to support decision-makers.
